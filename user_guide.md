# User Guide: Jaxtra Payroll System

Welcome to the **Jaxtra Payroll System**, your all-in-one solution for managing employees, attendance, loans, and payroll with ease. This guide provides a high-level overview of how to navigate and use the system's key features.

## 1. Dashboard Overview
The **Dashboard** is your mission control. It provides a quick bird's-eye view of your business:
- **Total Employees**: How many people are on your team.
- **Attendance Summary**: A snapshot of today's attendance.
- **Financial Status**: Total loans/kasbon pending and recent payroll summaries.

## 2. Managing Employees (Karyawan)
The foundation of the system is the **Data Karyawan** module.
- **Add/Edit**: Store employee names, positions, and salary rates.
- **Salary Types**: Choose between **Harian** (Daily) or **Bulanan** (Monthly) rates for each employee.
- **Mobile Cards**: On your phone, switch to card view for quick edits while on the floor.

## 3. Tracking Attendance (Absensi)
Mark attendance daily to ensure accurate payroll.
- **Hadir/Tidak Hadir**: Simply tap the status button to toggle. A pulsing green dot indicates they are present.
- **Overtime (Lembur)**: Record extra hours worked directly in the attendance table.
- **Notes**: Add optional comments (e.g., "Sakit dengan surat dokter") to explain absences.

## 4. Managing Loans (Kasbon)
Handle employee loan requests and track repayments effortlessly.
- **Add Loan**: Record a new loan amount for any employee.
- **Track Status**: See "Total Pinjaman," "Sudah Dibayar," and the remaining "Sisa Kasbon" at a glance.
- **Manual Payment**: Record a payment if an employee pays back cash outside of the payroll cycle.
- **Red Coding**: Active debts are highlighted in **Red** so you never lose track.

## 5. Payroll Pipeline (Penggajian)
The core of the app—calculating and finalizing salaries.
- **Calculate**: Pick a date range (e.g., 1st to 30th) and hit "Hitung Gaji." The system automatically factors in days present and overtime hours.
- **Loan Deduction**: The system shows how much loan (kasbon) is available to be deducted. You can manually enter a deduction amount.
- **Confirm**: Once finalized, hit "Konfirmasi." This permanently updates the employee's loan balance.
- **Export**: Generate professional **Excel** reports for your records or download individual **PDF Slips** for your employees.

## 6. Admin & Branding
For system administrators:
- **Custom Branding**: Upload your company logo for the Login page, Header, and PDF Slips.
- **Adjust Sizes**: Fine-tune how large the logo appears in each section.
